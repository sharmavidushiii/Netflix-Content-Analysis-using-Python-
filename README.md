📊 Netflix Content Analysis: Exploring Global Entertainment Trends
📌 Project Overview

This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset using Python. The goal is to uncover insights into Netflix's content library, including content distribution, genre trends, country-wise production, ratings, and content growth over time.

Through data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, and analysis, this project demonstrates practical data analytics skills and data-driven storytelling.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows on Netflix.
Identify the top content-producing countries.
Explore popular genres and content ratings.
Examine Netflix's content growth over the years.
Analyze movie duration patterns.
Generate actionable insights from the dataset.

📈 Key Insights
Movies make up the majority of Netflix's content library.
The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
Drama and International Movies are among the most common genres.
Netflix experienced significant content growth after 2015.
TV-MA is one of the most frequently assigned content ratings.
Most movies have a duration between 80 and 120 minutes.
